Preparing to Enter the Holy Temple AKA Temple Prep

I have been preparing to enter the temple, and I am so excited but also nervous. The way I have been preparing to go is by going to temple prep and reading Preparing to Enter the Holy Temple. This is a booklet that is used in temple prep. It is a booklet adapted from the book The Holy Temple, by Boyd Kay Packer.
These are things that stood out to me, and that I had impressions on.

Come to the Temple

“In the Church we build buildings pf many kinds. In them we worship, we teach, we find recreation, we organize. We can organize stakes and wards, and missions and quorums and Relief Societies in these buildings or even in rented halls. But, when we organize families according to the order that the Lord has revealed, we organize them in the temples. Temple marriage, that sealing ordnance, is a crowning blessing, that you may claim in the holy temple.” (Page 1:Paragraph 2)

I think it is neat that they word this as organizing the family. I never thought of it that way, and it is true. The Lord organizes everything, even if you don’t realize it. When I think about it, I realized that the Lord has organized a lot of things. The Nephites, the people of Babylon, and the twelve tribes of Israel, even the different age groups that you have when going to church. What else can you think of that the Lord has organized?

"These Things are Sacred"

“Those who have been to the temple have been taught an ideal: Someday every living soul and every soul who has ever lived shall have the opportunity to hear the gospel and to accept or reject what the temple offers. If this opportunity is rejected, the rejection must be I the part of the individual.”

The only one who can say no to the gospel is themselves. The Lord and Jesus are wiling to let anyone accept and come into the kingdom of God. If they are willing to accept the truths that they are shown. If they are not that is on them, and we have no room to judge.
